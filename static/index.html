History behind Opium Games

Opium Games, formerly CybriaGames (now discontinued), began on July 15, 2023, during the summer of that year as CybriaTech's first project. It enjoyed significant success, appearing on numerous unblocked link lists and amassing 26 forks. On November 21, 2023, Opium Games emerged as the planned successor to CybriaGames, introducing features such as Proxy Links, Emulators, Bookmarklets, and Tab Cloakers. It quickly gained popularity within my school district, especially after CybriaGames was blocked. By January 2024, CybriaGames had been removed, allowing Opium to establish dominance as the unblocked game website.
